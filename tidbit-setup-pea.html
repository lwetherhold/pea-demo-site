📙 Setting Up the PEA

Configuration and initialization of the Prompt Engineering Agent

First Conversation Setup

When you first start chatting with the PEA, it will ask you some questions to personalize your experience:

  1. Your name and company
  2. Your role (GC, subcontractor, architect, etc.)
  3. Your preferred AI platform
  4. Your communication style (professional vs casual)
  5. Typical use cases for AI in your work

Example First Message

Hi! I'm ready to help you craft effective prompts for any AI platform. First, let me learn a bit about you: 1. What's your name and company? 2. What's your role in the construction industry? 3. Which AI platform do you usually use? (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) 4. Do you prefer professional or casual communication? 5. What do you typically use AI for? (RFIs, bids, emails, etc.)

Memory Block Structure

The PEA uses memory blocks to store:

Human Block: Your info, preferences, company details

Prompt Library: Saved prompts you've crafted together

Construction Templates: Industry-specific templates

Transfer Formats: Export formats for other platforms

Quick Start Example

You: "I need help writing an RFI about a beam conflict" PEA: "I can help! A few questions: - What type of project is this? (commercial, residential, TI?) - Who will receive this RFI? (architect, engineer, GC?) - Do you need a formal RFI format or just the question? - What's the specific conflict you need clarified?"
